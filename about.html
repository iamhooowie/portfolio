About

Hello! My name is Howie. I’m from Taiwan, and I’m glad you visited my website! I am a product designer fascinated by the roles of design and technology in people’s interactions with everyday objects. I have a passion for creating memorable and delightful digital experiences for users. Planning, designing, problem-solving, and front-end development give me a sense of fulfillment, and this virtuous cycle makes me love my job.

In my spare time, I like to improve my skills to stay competitive. I also enjoy learning about the cultural patterns of different countries. Given the different lifestyles in various countries, this will enable me to broaden my horizons and stimulate my ideas. In addition, I enjoy photography and painting, which continue to inspire my ideas about aesthetics.
