About

Hi! I'm Howie, a product designer from Taiwan currently based in Boston. I design digital products that make complex things simple and bring ideas to life through a mix of user research, visual design, and collaboration. Over the past 6 years, I've worked on 100+ projects across education, government, and civic technology in Taiwan—including an educational game that reached 100,000+ elementary students and was adopted as official curriculum. What excites me most about design is the intersection of creativity and problem-solving. I love digging into user research, building design systems, and collaborating with engineers to create experiences that feel intuitive and delightful.

Outside of work, you'll find me exploring Boston with my camera, observing how people interact with spaces and each other. I'm currently pursuing my Master at Northeastern University, constantly learning and growing as a designer. Feel free to explore my work, and let's connect if you'd like to chat about design, Taiwan, or anything in between!
